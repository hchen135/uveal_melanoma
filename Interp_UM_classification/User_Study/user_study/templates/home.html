Introduction

User study for Uveal Melanoma prognostication through cytopatholohy images

In this study, you will be shown several UM cytopathological images aiming to diagnose UM prognostication (GEP class 1 like/GEP class 2 like or lower/higher metastatic risk) in different situations.

This study contains 3 main tasks. You are asked to prognose UM for 6 specimens in each task. Descriptions and instructions of each task will be introduced later. The entire process is supposed to be less than 1 hour.

Tasks are:

  • Diagnose with images only.
  • Diagnose with images and AI recommendation.
  • Diagnose with images, AI recommendation and AI extracted information.
